1. Purpose

This document records the AI objectives Cloudax sets for its AI Management System, in accordance with Clause 6.2 of ISO/IEC 42001:2023. It establishes both standing objectives (which persist across cycles) and annual KPIs (which are reviewed and refreshed each year).

ISO 27001:2022 references: ISO 42001 reference: Clause 6.2 AI objectives and planning to achieve them

2. Scope

These objectives apply to the full AIMS scope as defined in AIMS-SCOPE-001. They are consistent with the AI Policy (AI-POL-001) and the AI Risk Treatment Plan (AIMS-RTP-001).

3. Roles and Responsibilities

  • AI Governance Lead: owns this document; tracks objective achievement; reports to management review.
  • Chief Technology Officer: contributes to setting and achieving technical objectives.
  • Privacy Lead: contributes to privacy-related objectives.
  • Chief Executive Officer: approves objectives at issue and review.

4. Standing AI Objectives

The following objectives apply continuously and are not refreshed annually:

100% of in-scope providers on AI-REG-3P-001 with current assessment.

100% of datasets recorded in AI-REG-DATA-001.

O-6

100% of AI incidents in AI-REG-INC-001 with closed-loop lessons.

6. Methodology

Objectives and KPIs are SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound). Each is owned by a named role with a clear measure. Measurement evidence is collected as part of the AIMS Compliance Monitoring Schedule (AI-PROG-COMP-001).

7. Review and Reporting

  • Standing objectives are reviewed at least annually for continued relevance.
  • Annual KPIs are reviewed at least every six months and refreshed annually.
  • Objective and KPI status is reported to AIMS management review.
  • Material under-performance triggers a corrective action under REG-CAR-001.

8. Resource Allocation

Resources required to achieve these objectives — personnel time, tooling, training budget, third-party assessment costs — are determined annually and approved by the Chief Executive Officer as part of the AIMS budget cycle.

9. Approval

These objectives are approved by the Chief Executive Officer at issue and at every annual refresh.

10. Maintenance

This document is reviewed at least annually. Standing objectives change rarely; annual KPIs are refreshed each year.
