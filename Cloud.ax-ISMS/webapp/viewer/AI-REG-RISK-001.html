1. Purpose

This AI Risk Register is the authoritative record of AI-related risks identified by Cloudax under the AI Risk Assessment Methodology (AIMS-RA-001). It is the operational basis for the AI Risk Treatment Plan (AIMS-RTP-001) and reporting to AIMS management review.

ISO 27001:2022 references: ISO 42001 reference: Clause 6.1.2 AI risk assessment; Clause 8.2 AI risk assessment

2. Scope

This Register covers all AI risks within the AIMS scope as defined in AIMS-SCOPE-001.

3. Definitions

Definitions are per AIMS-RA-001 §3.

4. Roles and Responsibilities

  • AI Governance Lead: owns this Register; ensures completeness; coordinates risk reviews.
  • Risk owners: assigned to each risk; responsible for ensuring treatment is effective.
  • Chief Technology Officer: contributes technical risks and treatment options.
  • Privacy Lead: contributes privacy-dimension risks.

5. How to Use This Register

  • Each risk has a unique identifier (AI-R-NNN).
  • Status values: Open, In treatment, Treated, Accepted, Closed.
  • This document holds a representative set of worked examples; the live register is maintained as an Excel workbook with the same column structure and is the authoritative source.
  • Review cadence is risk-band-driven (Section 8).

6. Risk Categories (cross-reference to AIMS-RA-001)

Categories per AIMS-RA-001 §6: Performance and accuracy; Bias and fairness; Privacy and data protection; Security; Transparency and explainability; Autonomy and oversight; Lawful and ethical; Societal and environmental; Operational; Third-party.

8. Review Cadence

  • Critical: quarterly + reported to every management review.
  • High: quarterly.
  • Moderate: annually.
  • Low: annually.

9. Reporting

  • Status of all High and Critical risks is reported to AIMS management review at least every six months.
  • Material new risks are reported promptly between cycles.
  • Trends across categories are reviewed at annual review.

10. Maintenance

  • This Register is updated continuously.
  • This document structure is reviewed at least annually.
