1. Purpose

This Management Review Log documents the management reviews of the Cloudax ISMS in accordance with Clause 9.3 of ISO/IEC 27001:2022. It serves both as a procedure for conducting the review and as the cumulative record of reviews held.

ISO 27001:2022 references: Cl. 9.3 Management review; Cl. 10.1 Continual improvement

2. Scope

This Log covers all formal management reviews of the Cloudax ISMS within the scope defined in ISMS-SCOPE-001.

3. Definitions

  • Management review: a top-management activity to ensure the continuing suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness of the ISMS.
  • Decision: a documented choice arising from review, with a named owner and target date.

4. Cadence

  • Formal management reviews are held at least once per twelve-month cycle.
  • Additional reviews are convened where material change occurs (e.g. major incident, major regulatory change, significant business change).
  • A short interim review is held at six months to track the annual objectives (ISMS-OBJ-001) and the Risk Treatment Plan (ISMS-RTP-001).

5. Inputs to Management Review

In line with Clause 9.3.2, every review considers the following standing inputs:

  • Status of actions from previous management reviews.
  • Changes in external and internal issues relevant to the ISMS.
  • Changes in needs and expectations of interested parties.
  • Feedback on information security performance, including:
    • — Nonconformities and corrective actions (REG-CAR-001).
    • — Monitoring and measurement results (PROG-COMP-001).
    • — Audit results (PROG-AUDIT-001).
    • — Fulfilment of information security objectives (ISMS-OBJ-001).
  • Feedback from interested parties — customer assurance findings, supplier issues, regulator interactions, personnel feedback.
  • Risk assessment and risk treatment status (REG-RISK-001, ISMS-RTP-001).
  • Opportunities for continual improvement.
  • Material incidents since the previous review (PLAN-IRP-001 records).
  • Status of the SoA and any proposed changes (ISMS-SOA-001).
  • Status of supplier landscape (Tier 1/2 changes, sub-processors).
  • Resource adequacy.

6. Outputs of Management Review

In line with Clause 9.3.3, every review records the following outputs:

  • Decisions on opportunities for continual improvement.
  • Decisions on changes to the ISMS, including changes to scope, policies, controls, or the SoA.
  • Resource decisions.
  • Confirmation (or revision) of risk acceptance for material residual risks.
  • New, revised, or closed actions, with owners and target dates, recorded in REG-CAR-001 where appropriate.

7. Roles and Responsibilities

  • Chair: Chief Executive Officer.
  • Secretariat: Information Security Manager — prepares papers, maintains this Log, tracks actions.
  • Standing attendees: CEO, Chief Technology Officer, Information Security Manager, Privacy Lead.
  • Invited attendees: internal auditor (where reporting), external advisers as required.

8. Standing Agenda

  • Welcome and review of previous actions.
  • Context update — external/internal issues, interested parties.
  • Performance — monitoring results, KPIs, objectives status, audit findings, customer assurance.
  • Risk — register update, treatment plan progress, residual acceptance.
  • Incidents — lessons since previous review.
  • Supplier landscape.
  • SoA and policy framework — proposed changes.
  • Continual improvement — opportunities.
  • Resource and budget.
  • Decisions and actions.

11. Decision and Action Tracking

  • Actions arising from review are recorded in REG-CAR-001 Corrective Actions Register where they relate to nonconformity or improvement.
  • Strategic actions (e.g. resource decisions, scope changes) are tracked separately by the Information Security Manager and reported back at the next review.
  • Status of all actions is reviewed at the start of every management review.

12. Records and Retention

  • Management review records are documented information required by Cl. 9.3.3.
  • Records are retained per POL-009 Records Retention Policy — minimum 6 years from review date.
  • Records are made available to internal and external auditors on request.

13. Maintenance

This Log structure (Sections 1–8, 10–11) is reviewed at least annually. The cumulative record (Section 9) is appended after every review and is not subject to version increment.

14. Acknowledgement

All standing attendees acknowledge this Log on commencement, on material change to the structure, and on request.
