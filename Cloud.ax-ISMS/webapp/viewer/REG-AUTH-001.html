1. Purpose

This Authority Contacts register records the relevant authorities, regulators, and special-interest groups with which Cloudax Ltd. maintains contact, in support of A.5.5 Contact with authorities and A.5.6 Contact with special interest groups.

ISO 27001:2022 references: Linked controls: A.5.5 Contact with authorities; A.5.6 Contact with special interest groups

2. Scope

This Register covers contacts relevant to Cloudax’s information security, data protection, business continuity, legal, and regulatory obligations within the UK.

3. Roles and Responsibilities

  • Information Security Manager: owns this Register; engages authorities under the Information Security Policy.
  • Privacy Lead: lead point of contact with the ICO.
  • Chief Executive Officer: ultimate authority for engagement with authorities and external advisers.

4. How to Use This Register

  • Use these contacts where the Information Security Policy, an incident playbook, or applicable law requires engagement.
  • Cloudax personnel must not engage external authorities outside their formal role without authorisation. Refer questions to the Privacy Lead, Information Security Manager, or Chief Executive Officer as appropriate.
  • Personal contacts replace the placeholders below; specific names and direct lines are kept in the live register held by the Information Security Manager.

8. Internal Escalation

In an incident requiring authority engagement, internal escalation is:

  • Information Security Manager assesses and notifies.
  • Privacy Lead consulted on personal data implications.
  • Chief Executive Officer informed of any external authority engagement, and authorises material commitments.
  • Personnel must not engage authorities directly without authorisation, except where required by personal legal duty.

9. Maintenance

  • This Register is reviewed at least annually by the Information Security Manager and the Privacy Lead.
  • Contact details are validated through low-stakes engagement at least annually (e.g. phone-tree confirmation, email round-trip).
  • Changes are recorded promptly.
