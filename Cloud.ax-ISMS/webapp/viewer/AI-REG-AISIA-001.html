1. Purpose

This Register holds the index of every AI System Impact Assessment (AISIA) Cloudax has conducted. The full AISIA records (the assessments themselves) are held alongside this index in the AIMS document repository.

ISO 27001:2022 references: ISO 42001 reference: A.5.3 Documentation of AI system impact assessments; A.5.2 AI system impact assessment process

2. Scope

This Register covers every AISIA conducted under AI-POL-004 and AI-PRO-002, for any AI system within the AIMS scope.

3. Definitions

Definitions are per AI-POL-004 §3.

4. Roles and Responsibilities

  • AI Governance Lead: owns this Register; signs off completed AISIAs; ensures the index is current.
  • Project / feature owners: lead the AISIA for their AI system; the resulting record is added to this Register.
  • Privacy Lead: contributes data subject impact perspective.

5. How to Use This Register

  • Each AISIA has a unique identifier (AISIA-NNN).
  • AISIA records persist permanently — superseded AISIAs are kept with status ‘Superseded’.
  • A current AISIA must exist for each in-scope AI system; this Register is the easy way to confirm that.

6. Register Schema

Each AISIA entry contains:

  • AISIA reference (AISIA-NNN).
  • AI system reference (AI-SYS-NNN, linked to AI-REG-INV-001).
  • Trigger for the AISIA.
  • Depth (Light / Standard / Enhanced).
  • Date completed.
  • Lead (named individual).
  • Residual impact band (Low / Moderate / High / Critical).
  • Acceptance authority (AI Governance Lead / CEO).
  • Cross-references (DPIA, AI Risk Register entries, related incidents).
  • Review / refresh date.
  • Status (Current / Superseded).

8. Relationship with DPIA (REG-DPIA-001)

  • AISIA covers AI-specific impact (bias, transparency, autonomy, societal effects).
  • DPIA covers data protection-specific impact under UK GDPR Article 35.
  • Where an AI system processes personal data, both apply; cross-references are maintained.
  • A combined AISIA + DPIA record is acceptable provided both standards’ requirements are met.

9. Review Cadence

  • Each AISIA has its own review date (Section 6).
  • Out-of-cycle review on material change to the AI system or following an incident.
  • Full register review annually for completeness.

10. Maintenance

  • This Register is updated as AISIAs are completed, refreshed, or superseded.
  • This document structure is reviewed at least annually.
